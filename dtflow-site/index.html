How it works

From file to film in six steps.

No tutorials needed. The panel walks you through each stage.

1

Load your design

Open Illustrator. Click Load Image in the DTFlow panel. Pick a PNG or JPG — it's placed on your active artboard.

client-logo.png
2

Trace

Choose a preset, drag the threshold if needed, hit Trace. Then Expand & Ungroup to get clean editable paths.

raster → vector
3

Clean

Pick the background colour with one click and remove every matching path. Swap black ↔ white if you're printing on dark fabric.

CLEAN
4

Preview on fabric

Drop a black or white background behind the art. Spot any transparency issues before they cost you film.

on black
5

Export

Type the exact width in cm, pick DPI, tick mirror, hit Export DTF PNG. Transparent background, exact pixels.

25 cm @ 300
6

Print

Send the PNG to your DTF printer. Press onto the garment. Done. The whole prep cycle — usually 10 to 20 minutes — now takes 30 seconds.
